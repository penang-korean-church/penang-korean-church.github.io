## Welcome to Penang Korean Church

Penang Korean Church is a vibrant Korean-speaking community located in Georgetown, Penang, Malaysia. We welcome all Korean residents, travelers, and anyone interested in worshiping with us.

Since 2010, our church has been a spiritual home for Koreans living in Penang and the surrounding areas. We are committed to worship, biblical teaching, community fellowship, and local outreach.

## Getting Here

### By Car
- From Georgetown city center: approximately 10 minutes
- From Penang International Airport: approximately 20 minutes
- Free parking available (30 spaces)

### By Public Transport
- Bus routes: 101, 102, 103
- Get off at "Jalan Burma" stop
- 5-minute walk from bus stop

### By Grab/Taxi
- Search for "Penang Korean Church" or use the address above
- Approximately RM 10-15 from Georgetown center
